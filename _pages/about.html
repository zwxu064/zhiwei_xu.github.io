I specialize in computer vision, machine learning, and image processing. Over the years, I have received my PhD in computer vision and machine learning from the Australian National University and Data61, CSIRO. I also hold a Master of Engineering from Xidian University and a Bachelor of Engineering from Huaqiao University. To the present, I have had the honor of being supervised by and collaborating with Prof. Richard Hartley, Prof. Stephen Gould, Dr. Thalaiyasingam Ajanthan, Prof. Lei Zhang, Prof. Mengdao Xing, Prof. Gui Feng, etc.
